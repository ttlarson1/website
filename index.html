How I can help

As a seasoned professional in the field of mental and physical health, I have had the honor of working with a diverse range of patients - from young adults to seniors.

My ultimate goal is to empower and guide clients towards overcoming depression and anxiety as well as other challenges in mood that may be standing in the way of your mental and emotional well-being.

Regardless of your age or the difficulties you may be facing, my unwavering focus remains on providing you with the tools and support necessary to lead a successful and joyous life.

Weight loss and hormone imbalance can also present challenges in physical and mental well-being. Not only can hormone imbalance plague your mental health as we age, it can also increase our risk for cardiac disease, bone loss, and cognitive changes. Increased weight, as we all know, can also have effects in cardiovascular disease, but can can worsen fatigue, endurance, and play a major role in self-esteem.

Initial Psychiatric Evaluation Intake

MN patients with Aetna, UnitedHealth/Optum, Cigna, and Out-of-Network Patients


This appointment addresses current symptoms, history, and goals of care. This will help outline a personalized treatment plan. When signing up please fill out the intake paperwork sent to your email prior to the appointment along with any recent lab tests, vital signs, weight, and any other information that you feel is necessary. This will help give us more time to focus on getting to know each other, treatment preferences, and options. Payment is due at the time of booking.

60 minutes

Follow-Up Appointments (Established Patients Only)

MN patients with Aetna, UnitedHealth/Optum, Cigna, and Out-of-Network Patients


Regular follow-up appointments are important to; track treatments, adjust medications, monitor refills, and check in on your progress. Therapy may also be available at these appointments.

30 minutes
