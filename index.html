How I Can Help You Feel Like You Again


You don’t have to navigate this alone. Whether you're feeling overwhelmed by anxiety, stuck in depression, weighed down by fatigue, or simply not like yourself, I’m here to help you reconnect with your strength, clarity, and peace of mind.

As a dual-certified Psychiatric Mental Health and Family Nurse Practitioner, I bring both clinical expertise and a compassionate, integrative approach to care. I’ve had the privilege of supporting individuals at every stage of life — from young adults facing emotional stress to older adults navigating hormonal changes and life transitions. No matter where you’re starting, my focus is always on helping you feel well in both mind and body.

Emotional struggles like depression or anxiety often have deeper roots — sometimes tied to hormone imbalances, weight fluctuations, sleep disruption, or chronic stress. These underlying issues can quietly impact your mental clarity, energy, self-esteem, and long-term health. Left untreated, they can affect everything from heart health to memory to quality of life.

Rather than offering a one-size-fits-all solution, I take time to understand your story, your values, and your goals. From there, I create a care plan that aligns with your preferences — whether that includes medications, supplements, hormone support, therapy, lifestyle guidance, or a more natural path forward.

You deserve care that sees the whole you — and I’d be honored to walk beside you on the journey to feeling vibrant, grounded, and truly yourself again.

Initial Psychiatric Evaluation Intake

Aetna, UnitedHealthcare/Optum, Cigna, BlueCross, and Out-of-Network coverage


This comprehensive intake appointment is your first step toward a personalized, integrative treatment plan. During our visit, we’ll review your current symptoms, medical and mental health history, and your goals for care. This time is dedicated to understanding you — so we can create a plan that aligns with your needs and preferences.

60 minutes

Follow-Up Appointments (Established Patients Only)

Aetna, UnitedHealthcare/Optum, Cigna, BlueCross, and Out-of-Network coverage


Ongoing care is key to achieving lasting progress. Follow-up appointments allow us to assess how your treatment is working, make any necessary adjustments to medications or supplements, review refill needs, and support your overall well-being. These sessions may also include brief therapy, depending on your needs and preferences.

30 minutes
